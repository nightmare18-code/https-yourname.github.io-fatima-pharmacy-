💚 Our Services – Caring for You, Every Step of the Way

At Fatima Pharmacy, we go beyond just providing medicines — we’re your trusted health partner, committed to your well-being and peace of mind. Our goal is to make healthcare easy, affordable, and accessible for every family.

  • 💊 Prescription Medicines: 100% genuine and certified medicines from trusted pharmaceutical brands.
  • 🌿 Health & Wellness Products: Vitamins, supplements, herbal remedies, skincare, and hygiene items.
  • 👶 Baby Care Essentials: Diapers, formula, oils, lotions, and more from reliable brands.
  • 💗 Personalized Health Advice: Expert guidance from our friendly pharmacists for better healthcare decisions.
  • 🚚 Home Delivery: Fast and reliable delivery service in Baldia Town and nearby areas.
  • 🩺 Health Check & Monitoring: Basic checks like blood pressure, sugar level, and temperature monitoring.
  • 👨‍⚕️ Home Doctor Visit: A certified doctor visits your home for checkups and consultations.
